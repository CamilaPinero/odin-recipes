Lasagna Recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat Sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients
  2. Spread 1 heaping cup of meat sauce
  3. Add 4 lasagna noodles
  4. Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray
  5. Bake for 35 minutes
